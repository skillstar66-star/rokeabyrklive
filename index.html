Perfect Fit

How We Measure

Every detail matters. We take precise measurements to craft a blouse that fits you like a second skin.

Blouse Length

From the highest point of shoulder down to the desired waist level.

Chest / Bust

Around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape horizontal.

Waist

Around the natural waistline where the blouse will end.

Shoulder

From the edge of one shoulder across the back to the other edge.

Sleeve Length

From the shoulder seam down to your preferred sleeve end.

Sleeve Width

Around the arm at the level where the sleeve ends.

Front Neck

From the shoulder neck point diagonally down to the desired front depth.

Back Neck

From the shoulder neck point down to the desired depth at the back.
